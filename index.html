technology

Luna sound system enables a even more layered and immersive stereo listening experience with multi-speaker pairing technology. With one single speaker you can only enjoy the best sound quality in front of the device. On the other hand, traditional stereo system costs tons of money. But today thanks to wireless pairing tech, we can expand the audio experience into stereo groups you can easily afford. Luna applies MESHNET technology, with which you can add up several Luna together to customize your own stereo system easily.
